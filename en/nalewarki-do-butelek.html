Bottle Filling Machines

Automatic filler with capper

Designed and manufactured in Poland. Fully integrated with a capping module. Built to fill bottles (plastic, glass, etc.), as well as tubes, jars and trays. The machine delivers high dosing precision, full process repeatability, and can run in line with a labeler. An ideal solution for companies that want to increase output and reduce labor costs.

Applications in filling automation

  • cosmetics (shampoos, gels, conditioners, oils, varnishes, liquid soaps, gels and antibacterial products),
  • household chemicals (window cleaners, concentrates, detergents),
  • food products (thick butters, spirits, oils, purées, dietary supplements),
  • products for pharmaceutical use.

Expansion options

You can extend the filler with the rest of the automation elements for your production line: a dosing pump, a cap or cork feeder, rotary tables (infeed, buffer, outfeed), and an automatic labeler with an optional printer.
