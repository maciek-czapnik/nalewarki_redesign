About us

KABP is an engineering company based in Kraków that has been passionately building production machines since 2017 – from the first sketch to the last screw. We design, engineer and assemble in Poland, because we believe local quality is both a global advantage and a long-term investment.

We are a manufactory, not a corporation. A team of people who genuinely enjoy what they do. Every machine we build is designed with a specific product, process and the person who will work with it in mind. What unites us is craftsman's precision, engineering imagination, and respect for technology that should serve people, not dominate them.

If you're looking for a partner who not only understands production, but understands you too – you're in the right place.

How we work

Our process

While every project is different, we usually work together in three steps.

1. Consultation

We talk through your product, packaging and production process to understand exactly what you need.

2. Design

We prepare a concept and design for a machine tailored to your process before anything reaches the shop floor.

3. Delivery

We build, assemble and commission the equipment ourselves, then help you put it to work in your production.

Experience

Quality and experience

We've been designing and assembling machines in Poland since 2017. In that time we've designed and built more than 50 machines that are now running in our clients' production facilities. Each one was built from the ground up – designed, engineered and assembled by our own team.
