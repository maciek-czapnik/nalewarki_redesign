Cookies

The Site does not automatically collect any information except for information contained in cookies. Cookies are IT data, in particular text files, stored on the User's end device, used when browsing the Site's webpages. Cookies usually contain the name of the website they originate from, their storage time on the end device, and a unique number.

Cookies are used to:

  • adjust the Site's content to the User's preferences and optimize the use of the webpages;
  • generate statistics that help us understand how Users use the webpages;
  • maintain the User's session.

The Site may use both "session" cookies (stored until the User logs out, leaves the Site, or closes the browser) and "persistent" cookies (stored for a period defined in their parameters, or until deleted by the User). The cookies used fall into four categories: "necessary" (required for the Site to function properly and cannot be disabled), "analytical" (allowing us to collect information on how the Site is used), "functional" (remembering choices made by the User), and "advertising" (used only with consent given in browser settings). Users can change their cookie settings at any time in their web browser's settings.
