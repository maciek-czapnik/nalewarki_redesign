Custom Solutions

Equipment designed around your production process

Not every production process fits a standard catalog machine. We design and build custom equipment – from a single atypical machine to the integration of several processes into one line – drawing on our own in-house design and manufacturing capability, from first sketch to final assembly.

When a custom solution fits

Not every production process fits a standard catalog machine. A custom solution is worth considering when your product, packaging or process doesn't match the standard, when you want to combine several production steps into a single machine, or when you're working with low volumes or an unusual packaging format.

How we work

We start with a conversation about your product and process to understand what's actually needed. We then prepare a concept and design for the equipment, build and assemble it in-house, and test it before handing it over for use in your production.

Scope of possibilities

Depending on your needs, a custom solution might include:

  • a single machine designed for a specific, non-standard package or product,
  • integrating several production steps (e.g. filling, capping, labeling) into one line,
  • adapting an existing solution to non-standard formats or low volumes,
  • prototype or pre-production equipment.
